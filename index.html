Made for iPad too

On iPad it all sits on one screen — your expenses, the category donut, and the monthly picture together. Tap a month to focus it, tap a category to filter, open analytics for the full story.

Yours, and only yours

Everything you put into Moniefest stays in Apple's on-device storage. The app has no networking code at all — no account, no cloud, no analytics, no ads. Your spending is nobody's business but yours, and it never leaves your phone.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Spendly Log does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data — of any kind, ever.

What the app does

Spendly Log is an expense-tracking app that stores everything you enter — expenses, categories, notes, and imported CSV data — locally on your device using Apple's on-device SwiftData storage. The app makes no network requests of any kind.

Data collection

We do not collect any data. There is no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDK, and no account or sign-in of any kind. Nothing you enter into the app ever leaves your device.

CSV, screenshot, and video import

When you import a CSV file, a screenshot, or a screen recording of your bank app, it is read and parsed entirely on your device. Text recognition uses Apple's on-device Vision framework — no image, video, or text ever leaves your phone.

Verify it yourself

You don't have to take our word for it. Enable Settings → Privacy & Security → App Privacy Report on your device and use the app — iOS records every network contact an app makes, and Spendly Log's is empty. The app is also fully functional in airplane mode, and its App Store privacy label reads "Data Not Collected".

Third parties

Spendly Log does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Children's privacy

Since the app collects no data from anyone, this applies equally to users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes (for example, if a future version adds an optional cloud sync feature), this page will be updated and the change will be reflected in the app's release notes.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to mail@abhinav.se, or see the support page.

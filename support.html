Spendly Log support

Spendly Log tracks your expenses entirely on your device — no account, no cloud sync, no network access.

Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?
All expenses are stored locally on your device only. Nothing is uploaded anywhere. If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it — there is currently no backup or export feature beyond CSV import.

What CSV formats can I import?
The app auto-detects common bank and card export formats (comma, semicolon, or tab-delimited; US and EU number formats; several date formats). If your bank's export doesn't import correctly, email us a sample (with amounts redacted if you prefer) and we'll add support for it.

Does it support multiple currencies?
Amounts display using your device's locale currency. Multi-currency conversion isn't supported in this version.

Does it sync between my iPhone and iPad?
Not currently — each device keeps its own separate local data.
